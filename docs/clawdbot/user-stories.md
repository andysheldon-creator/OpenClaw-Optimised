# Clawdbot User Stories

## US-01: Weekly Website Performance Report

I'm a User, I want to use Google Analytics to see how well my website is doing,
I want my AI tool to look at that, then look at our most recent SEO updates and
give me a weekly analysis report.

## US-02: Sales Lead Follow-Up

I'm a Sales Manager, I want my AI tool to pull new leads from our CRM (HubSpot),
cross-reference them with LinkedIn company data, score them by fit, and send me
a daily Telegram summary of the top 5 leads I should follow up with today.

## US-03: Customer Support Ticket Triage

I'm a Support Lead, I want my AI tool to watch incoming Zendesk tickets, classify
them by urgency and topic, auto-reply to common questions using our knowledge base,
and escalate anything it can't handle to the right team member on Slack.

## US-04: Invoice Reconciliation

I'm a Finance Manager, I want my AI tool to pull invoices from our accounting
software (Xero/QuickBooks), match them against purchase orders in our procurement
system, flag mismatches, and generate a weekly reconciliation summary I can review
and approve through the dashboard.

## US-05: Social Media Content Calendar

I'm a Marketing Manager, I want my AI tool to analyze our top-performing posts
across Instagram, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn, identify content themes that drive
engagement, and draft a two-week content calendar with suggested copy and
posting times â€” then let me approve or edit each post before it goes live.

## US-06: Competitor Price Monitoring

I'm an E-commerce Manager, I want my AI tool to scrape competitor product pages
daily, compare their prices against our catalog, alert me on Telegram when a
competitor undercuts us by more than 10%, and suggest pricing adjustments with
projected margin impact.

## US-07: Employee Onboarding Checklist

I'm an HR Manager, I want my AI tool to trigger an onboarding workflow when a new
hire is added to our HRIS (BambooHR/Gusto), automatically create their accounts
(Google Workspace, Slack, GitHub), assign training modules, schedule their first
week of meetings, and send me a daily status dashboard showing what's complete.

## US-08: Infrastructure Health Digest

I'm a DevOps Engineer, I want my AI tool to pull metrics from Datadog/Grafana,
correlate them with recent deployments from our CI/CD pipeline (GitHub Actions),
identify anomalies or performance regressions, and send me a morning Slack digest
with root-cause hypotheses and recommended actions.

## US-09: Weekly Financial Dashboard

I'm a Small Business Owner, I want my AI tool to connect to my Stripe account
and my bank feed (Plaid), categorize transactions, calculate burn rate and runway,
compare actuals against my budget spreadsheet (Google Sheets), and deliver a
weekly financial snapshot to my email with trend charts and plain-English commentary.

## US-10: Legal Document Review Pipeline

I'm a Contracts Manager, I want my AI tool to watch a shared Google Drive folder
for new vendor contracts, extract key terms (payment terms, liability caps, renewal
dates, SLA commitments), compare them against our standard terms template, flag
deviations, and create a review task in our project management tool (Asana/Linear)
with a summary of what needs negotiation.
